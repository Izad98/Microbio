A LITTLE ABOUT US

Welcome to the world of microbiology! Microbio, is an exclusive blog which explores the latest microbiology trends and the industry. Throughout the journey, you will witness the trending fields and areas of consideration in the scientific world. Microbio consists of three segments which cover a diverse number of fields such as astromicrobiology, technological microbiology and microbial biotechnology. This was developed with the sole focus of providing scientific education and empowering science enthusiasts to step into the world of microbiology research.

Our characteristic blog article on astromicrobiology, is an interesting read on the role of microbes in evolution to colonizing Mars. The graphical poster is a descriptive journey to marine pollution and bioremediation with regards to Sri Lanka’s worst marine disaster from X-Press Pearl. Finally you will be having a digital experience on microbiology in various industries, energy applications and waste management. Microbio blog crew ensures to provide solid scientific literature along with steeping trends in the industry of microbiology in a way that you, a beginner, intermediate or an expert can learn with ease in a few minutes.
